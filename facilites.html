Our

 Facilities

Showcase of our facilities

Fully-Equipped Gym

Our state-of-the-art gym is fully equipped with the latest fitness equipment to meet all your training needs. Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned athlete, our facility offers a wide range of machines and free weights to help you achieve your fitness goals. In addition to cardio and strength training equipment, we provide a spacious area for functional workouts and stretching. Our dedicated fitness coaches are available to assist you with personalized training plans and to ensure you get the most out of your workout sessions.

Large, Matted Martial Arts Area

Our facility features a spacious, matted area specifically designed for martial arts training. The mats provide a safe and comfortable environment for practicing various disciplines, including jiu-jitsu, karate, judo, and Muay Thai. This area is ideal for both individual practice and group classes, ensuring that you have ample space to hone your skills and techniques.

Sauna / Steam Room

Unwind and relax in our luxurious sauna. The heat therapy offered by our sauna helps to soothe sore muscles, improve circulation, and promote overall relaxation. It's the perfect way to end a rigorous training session or to simply take a break from your daily routine. It's an excellent complement to your workout routine, helping you to detoxify and refresh your body and mind.

Changing and Shower Facilities

Our gym is equipped with modern changing and shower facilities to ensure your comfort and convenience. Spacious lockers are available to securely store your belongings, and our clean, well-maintained showers provide a refreshing end to your workout or training session. We strive to offer a comfortable and hygienic environment for all our members.
